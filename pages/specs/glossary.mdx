# Glossary

This glossary provides definitions for key terms and concepts used throughout the Sagal Framework Specification.

<div className="glossary">

## Clause
A fundamental unit of meaning in Sagal, analogous to a sentence in natural language, which is constructed using grammatical particles and content words to form executable code.

## Grammatical Particles
Linguistic constructs in Sagal that function similarly to articles, prepositions, and conjunctions in natural language.
They form the structural framework for defining the relationships between clauses in the code.

## Content Words
In Sagal, content words carry the primary meaning of actions, data, or entities in the program, similar to nouns and verbs in natural language.
