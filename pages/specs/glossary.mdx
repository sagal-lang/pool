# Glossary

This glossary provides definitions for key terms and concepts used throughout the Sagal Framework Specification.

<div className="glossary">

## Clause
The fundamental unit of meaning in Sagal, analogous to a sentence in natural language, that encapsulates executable logic.

## Grammatical Particles
Linguistic constructs in Sagal that function similarly to articles, prepositions, and conjunctions in natural language.
They govern the structure of clauses and define relationships between clauses, driving both the syntax and semantics of the code.
